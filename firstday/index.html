A Few Words about the College

AADIM NATIONAL COLLEGE is one of the finest urban colleges in Nepal established in 2071 located at the prime location of Kathmandu a walking distance from ring road nearby Chuchepati Petrol Pump. It is located in between big commercial complex KL TOWER & BHATBHATENI Supermarket. AADIM COLLEGE imparts the best available +2 program as per the curriculum and syllabus designed by National Examination Board (NEB), in Science, Management, Humanities, and Law. At the undergraduate level, the college offers 4 years of 8 semesters BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) & BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) degrees affiliated to TRIBHUVAN UNIVERSITY.
